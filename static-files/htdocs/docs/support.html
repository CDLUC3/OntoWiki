Listserv

The "udfr-l" listserv is available for members of the UDFR community to communicate with each other.

  • To subscribe, send the message "SUB UDFR-L name" to listserv@ucop.edu.
The list archive is available at http://listserv.ucop.edu/cgi-bin/wa.exe?A0=UDFR-L.
